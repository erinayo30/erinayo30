My Name And Birth


My names is Joshua Adeyemi Farinmade, I was born into the Family of Mr and Mrs Samuel A Arasanyin of the Ajaga ruling family of Afa Okeagbe on the 30th of January some years back.    I am from Okeagbe-Akoko in Akoko North west of Ondo State. I am a student of Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba Akoko, of the department of History And Internation studies 300level My best food is pounded yam with vegetable soup while my best color is white.

I am a lover of God gentle, friendly anxious and always ready to learn new thing.
